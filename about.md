    <h1 class="display-6 fw-bold mt-2 mb-4">About Brandon Travel Pages</h1>
    <p>Brandon Travel Pages is a structured reference site built for the travel intelligence side of Brandon Travel. Instead of publishing general travel advice, the site focuses on pages that organize airports, languages, time zones, visa systems, and mobility information into clear, search-friendly tables.</p>
    <p>The purpose is to create a middle layer between raw datasets and longer-form analysis. Datasets provide machine-readable structure. Insights and research explain what the data means. These pages make the information easier for people to browse quickly.</p>
    <p>The launch version includes ten high-value travel data pages that are realistic candidates for long-term organic search traffic while still fitting the Brandon Travel brand.</p>
